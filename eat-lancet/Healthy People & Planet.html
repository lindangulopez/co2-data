It is now recognized that food forms an inextricable link between human health and environmental sustainability. A planetary diet was established for the global food system to operate within boundaries for human health and food production to ensure healthy diets from sustainable food systems for nearly 10 billion people by 2050.

The planetary health diet outlines the food groups and ranges of food intakes, which combined in a diet, would optimize human and planetary health now it's up to us to whip us delicious food.

Five Strategies for a Great Food Transformation:

(1) Seek international and national commitment to shift toward healthy diets.

(2) Reorient agricultural priorities from producing high quantities of food to producing high quality food.

(3) Sustainably intensify food production to increase high-quality outputs.

(4) Strong and coordinated governance of land and oceans.

(5) At least halve food losses and waste, in line with UN Sustainable Development Goals
